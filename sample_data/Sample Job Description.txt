Data Scientist - Job Description

Company: Innovatech Solutions
Location: Remote / San Francisco, CA

About the Role:
We are looking for a talented and experienced Data Scientist to join our AI team.
You will design, build, and deploy machine learning and deep learning models that
power our core products. You will work closely with engineering, product, and
business teams to deliver data-driven solutions at scale.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and deploy machine learning and deep learning models in production
- Build and maintain NLP pipelines for text classification, entity extraction, and summarization
- Design ETL pipelines and work with large-scale data using Spark and Airflow
- Collaborate with backend engineers to integrate ML APIs into existing systems
- Monitor model performance and retrain models using MLOps best practices
- Communicate findings and model results to non-technical stakeholders using Tableau or Power BI
- Lead data science projects end-to-end and mentor junior team members

Required Skills & Qualifications:
- 4+ years of experience as a Data Scientist or Machine Learning Engineer
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras
- Experience with NLP techniques and frameworks (BERT, Transformers, spaCy, NLTK)
- Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms: regression, classification, clustering, ensemble methods
- Experience with cloud platforms: AWS (SageMaker, S3, EC2) or GCP
- Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines
- Experience with databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Nice to Have:
- Experience with MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar MLOps tools
- Knowledge of Apache Spark and distributed computing
- Contributions to open-source machine learning projects
- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity package
- Fully remote work option
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
